Research Goals

By writing about and conducting research on the L1, L2, and L3 landscapes, we aim to grow our understanding of the constantly changing innovation behind opportune protocols and networks. By encouraging this in-depth analysis we hope to solidify our member’s understanding of this space. This understanding should be to the extent they can competently navigate and utilize these networks on their own. While additionally, they develop the necessary foundational skills and knowledge to pursue a professional career in blockchain/cryptocurrency related fields.

Areas of Research

Through active management of the digital assets fund, investment proposals, and publishing informational research/personal pieces on our blog; members will have the opportunity to work autonomously to do their own research and grow their skillset. Areas of research are broad and encompass the entirety of blockchain based technology. However, research done through investment proposals or published blog content will be vetted and approved to ensure relevance and quality.
